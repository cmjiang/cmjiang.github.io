About

I am a Ph.D. candidate, major in Computer Science at University of California Davis. I am advised by Professor Zhaojun Bai. My research interests are in numerial algorithms and high performance computing. I got my M.S. and B.S. degree in Information Security at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

Recent Projects

  • FASTGE2
    Theoretical and computational solutions to eigenproblems of positive semi-definite pencils arising from data clustering.
    Kernel problem: \(L_Gx = \lambda L_H x\), where \(L_G\geq 0, L_H\geq 0, null(L_G)∩null(L_H)\neq\varnothing\).
    Package available in Matlab.
    Show details.

  • FSI
    A fast selected inversion algorithm for computing selected entries of p-cyclic matrices.
    Kernel problem: \(G=M^{-1}\), where \(M\) is a p-cyclic matrix.
    Package available in Fortran with multi-core/multi-node OpenMP/MPI hybrid implementation.
    Show details.

  • ICGSEP
    A reduction algorithm for solving ill-conditioned generalized symmetric eigenvalue problems.
    Kernel problem: \(Ax=\lambda Bx\), where \(A^T=A,B^T=B\geq 0\) w.r.t. a prescribed threshold \(\varepsilon\).
    Matlab function and LAPACK-style Fortran routine available.
