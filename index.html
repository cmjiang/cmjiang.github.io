About

I am a Ph.D. candidate, major in Computer Science at University of California Davis, advised by Professor Zhaojun Bai. My research interests are in numerial algorithms and high performance computing. I got my M.S. and B.S. degree in Information Security at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

Recent Projects

  • FAST-GE-2.0: A robust and efficient generalized spectral method for constrained clustering.

  • FSI: A fast selected inversion algorithm for computing selected entries of p-cyclic matrices.

  • xSYGVIC: A LAPACK-style package for solving ill-conditioned generalized symmetric eigenvalue problems.
