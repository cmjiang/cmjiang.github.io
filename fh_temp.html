ICGSEP

A solver for ill-conditioned generalized symmetric eigenvalue problems.

Description

  • ICGSEP implements the Fix-Heiberger reduction algorithm for solving ill-conditioned generalized symmetric eigenvalue problems in the form of

    \begin{equation}\label{eq:gsep} Ax = \lambda Bx \end{equation}

    where \(A\) and \(B\) are real symmetric matrices and \(B\) is positive semi-definite w.r.t. a threshold \(\varepsilon\).
